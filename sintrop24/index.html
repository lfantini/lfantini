This is the third meeting of the ANR group Aspects tropicaux des singularités (SINTROP).
The workshop will take place at the Centre de Mathématiques Laurent Schwartz, in the Campus of École Polytechnique, from Wednesday, March 20th to Friday, March 22nd, 2024.

Organizing committee: Lorenzo Fantini, Charles Favre.

Speakers

There will be a two minicourses, given by
  • Omid Amini
  • Vlerë Mehmeti
and regular talks by
  • André Belotto da Silva
  • Erwan Brugallé (to be confirmed)
  • Marco Maculan
  • Martin Ulirsch

If you are interested in attending the workshop you can get in touch with Lorenzo Fantini and/or Charles Favre.
